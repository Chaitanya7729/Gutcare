Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Solutions for All Your Dental Concerns

What dental services do you offer at Dr. Gowd's Dental Clinic?
We offer a wide range of services, including checkups, teeth whitening, braces, dental implants, root canals, and more.
Are your treatments covered by insurance?
Please contact our office with your insurance details, and we'd be happy to check your coverage.
How can I maintain oral hygiene after a dental procedure?
Follow the specific instructions provided by your dentist. Generally, this includes gentle brushing, flossing, and rinsing with an antiseptic mouthwash.
Do you offer emergency dental care?
Yes, we offer emergency dental care. Please call our office immediately if you are experiencing severe pain or trauma.
What are the signs I need to see a dentist?
Signs include persistent toothache, swollen gums, bleeding while brushing, sensitivity to hot or cold, or noticeable changes in your teeth or gums.
