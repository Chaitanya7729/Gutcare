Symptoms of Piles

  • ✔ A hard, possibly painful lump may be felt around the anus.
  • ✔ After passing a stool, a person with piles may feel the bowels are still full.
  • ✔ Bright red blood is visible after a bowel movement.
  • ✔ The area around the anus is itchy, red, and sore.
  • ✔ Pain occurs during the passing of a stool.
  • ✔ Excessive anal bleeding, possibly leading to anemia.
  • ✔ Fecal incontinence, or inability to control bowel movements.
  • ✔ Anal fistula, forming a new channel near the anus.
  • ✔ Leakage of feces.
